About Me

I am a Doctoral Researcher at Friedrich-Schiller-Universität, Jena, Germany, in the Theoretical Physics Department. My current research focuses on numerical simulations of magnetic field dynamics in neutron stars (see blog). I am interested in turbulence, magnetic fields, compact objects and dust in the ISM.

I was born in Bolzano, Italy, in 2001. I started high school in Italy and completed A-Levels in Devon, UK. I recently obtained a Master’s degree from the University of Bath, UK, after completing my project at the Nordic Institute of Theoretical Physics, Stockholm, Sweden. Mention outside interests?
